Bio

Raised up alongside a load of well influencing artists and musicians. Blaze love's music, have been off and on most my life. Big plans for the future for Blaze and now focusing on his EP which will be called "Unexpected." featuring Frilla & Mr Ess featuring all Frill Beats and will be 6 tracks
