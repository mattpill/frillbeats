Onesoul

Bio

A Bristol based Producer/Singer/Songwriter that has been producing Hip Hop, Soul, Jazz, Dance, and Electro for over 10 years.  Has worked with many Bristol based and international artists. Also played in a band called Syte and The Sound.


(SaTS) were a 6-piece live band from Bristol, UK. They played a powerful blend of music ranging from hip-hop, trip-hop, soul, rock, dnb, funk, dub and electronica – strong percussion, funky grooves, beautiful, poetic vocals and shimmering electronics.


Onesoul has performed all over UK with the likes of Dead Prez, Example, Ewan Hoozami, Buggsy.


Onesoul has created tracks and sang vocals for Buggsy, Frilla, Danderson, Handprint, DoMusic, Post Script, Jonny (Scribes), Da Hybrid, Social Conscience, Kamikaze, BEZ, Supreme of the arts, Ewan Hoozami and many more to come.


Onesoul is currently writing an album and singing/playing keys for a new Bristol based Hip Hop Band called Mechanical Bison Theory… (TBC). Onesoul has a range of original styles and a large array of beats/vocal harmonies that come naturally to him.
