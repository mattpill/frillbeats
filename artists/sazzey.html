Bio

SAZZEY comes from the great music city of Bristol. She started writing her own lyrics and beat boxing at school; finding it a great way to express her feelings and emotions. She performs regularly around the city at Open Mic nights, local pubs and festivals.


Now SAZZEY is currently working on her EP to be released in August this year 2015; she is very excited and cannot wait for people to hear the great material she has to coming. Recent performance have been at Bristol Pride, and the Sunfestival at Frampton Cotterell. Don’t worry if you miss her then you can see her at the Balloon Fiesta. It’s going to be a busy year for SAZZEY, now with the support from Frillbeats, a lot of performances and releases in Bristol and the UK are to follow check out the website for more info and to see if she’s in a venue near you !!!
