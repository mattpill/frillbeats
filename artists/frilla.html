Bio

Bristolian born and bred artist and producer who started making his own beats back in 1999 on his PS2. Since then he has advanced greatly and since 2005 has been song writing, Frilla's songs are based on his real life experiences so that the listener can relate and learn from his music. Previous performances include: Colston Hall (supporting KANO) Carling Academy Bristol (supporting GOLDIE LOOKING CHAIN) Lakota (supporting STYLES P) Club Capri (supporting Wayne Marshall) R.Y.M.A. Awards St Pauls Festival Bristol Harbour Side Festival Amaroundo Festival. ROLLDEEP, and also SWAY. With all this experience Frilla has grown as an artist and has a strong Bristol fan base behind him, his music is spreading every day, and has had the amazing opportunity to compete in the Vauxhall Tribes Urban Music Competition where he won "BEST MC" and supported Dizzee Rascal on his "Maths + English" UK tour in November 2007. and also supported Lethal Bizzle in March 2008 alongside this also has a track on The Bodysnatchers Album witch is doing very well also the track "My Appetite" is the 3rd most downloaded track on iTunes in america. From 2009 Frilla has 3 different style tracks on the Dj Woody Vs The Bodysnatchers mixtape which is also on iTunes. The future is looking very bright for Frilla, who is still in the process of creating more good quality music everyday on his projects & with his close family brothers "Statix, The Bodysnatchers, Shanti-Sounds, The Beatmonkeys, Werk Crew, Bristol, around the world & so on...


Also Has featured & produced music for CAPCOM on the 25th anniversary boxset and the MTV show Plain jain.


Now with all this great exciting experience behind him Frilla Is a Label Owner to help bristol and anyone who is serious about music progress


Frilla will allways be continuing to represent Bristol to the fullest extent.
