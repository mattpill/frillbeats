Bio

Jamie Searle (AKA Mr Ess) was born in Bristol on 27th January 1992.


From an early age Jamie was drawn into the world of music, highly influenced by old school rappers like 2Pac, Dr Dre, Snoop Dogg & Eminem he found he could relate to their music and later on followed in the footsteps of his idols. What first started off as a group of friends spitting bars in the park turned into a passion that he couldn't fight or resist.


Writing lyrics came naturally and with every verse came improvements which was noticeable in every track that was released upon the internet, at first he started writing grime music but realised he was versatile in every aspect of music whether it be grime, hip hop, dnb etc. Along his musical path while testing different genres of music he perfected his lyrics and flow which saw him grow. Thanks to this Jamie has been introduced to a few big names in Bristol, working closely alongside them improving his abilities and skills on the way.


Expect to see and hear more from this artist in the next coming months with E.Ps, singles, videos.
