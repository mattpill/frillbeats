Bio

Doctor Moody –Blowpop Bristol. a renowned producer, musician and DJ from Bristol UK, has built up his reputation and wowed the crowds in South Africa, Europe and America with his seamless mixing skills and jedi cuts and drops. His infectious stage show of decks, samplers, live vocals,live percussion and visuals combining the most underground of breaks and house with the funkiest party tunes has made him everyone’s favorite DJ.


His career has spanned out over the last 20 years and has seen him share the stage with some of the worlds most recognized DJ and artists at all major festivals. With releases on over 11 record labels, on all the major digital sites and a selection of vinyl 12" records.


Dr Moody Began working in the studio whilst just a teenager and has gone on to run his own broadcast quality studio ever since . This has seen him work on foley for prime time TV and score for film. Recording voice over artists for over 30 TVC's and Radio commercials, and compose music for BMW, Courvoisier and many others.The Doctor's studio also works for several record labels and artists, Mastering releases, With the Doctors 20 years experience you can always be sure of a good sound, work earlier this year reached the DnB arenas top ten. Always busy Always in time on time every time.
