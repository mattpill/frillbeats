Bio

Levi "Q Beats" Mchugh is a 21 year old up and coming Hip Hop/Grime Music Producer/Mc and Graphic Designer From Bristol , UK


He started out spitting for fun with his friends around his area from a young age.


As time went on he took it more seriously and wanted his own beats to spit over, trying to hook up with producers over Bristol many were Uninterested or not what he was Looking for, So he decided he was going to take a break from rapping to teach himself how to make beats.


During his Producing grind his work rate got him noticed by Frilla who recentely started up his own music label FRILL BEATS which Q is now apart of
