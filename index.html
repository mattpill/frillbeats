About

Frillbeats is a Bristol based record label lead by producer/MC Frilla.

We aim to provide a variety of musical output, focusing on house, 2-step, bassline, and covering everything in-between. Expect to see releases from Frilla, Game Changers, the Fruity House family and other up and coming Bristol Talent. Enjoy and embrace the unique vibes, sounds and tastes brought to you by Frillbeats.
